Welcome! This page swaps a single image depending on the user’s local time — morning, afternoon, or evening/night — using a few lines of JavaScript.

How it works: the script reads your device’s hour (0–23) and selects Morning (06:00–11:59), Afternoon (12:00–17:59), or Evening/Night (18:00–05:59). No personal data is collected; the time check runs only in your browser.
